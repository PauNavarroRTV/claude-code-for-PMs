## Example — AVOD Content Lists

| # | Category | User Persona | User Story | Functional Definition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | AVOD Content Lists | [UP] B2B2C Persona | AS A user, I WANT TO browse AVOD content presented in clear, visually engaging lists, SO THAT I can easily discover movies and series available to watch for free. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Pages & Lists |
| 2 | AVOD Content Lists | [UP] B2B2C Persona | AS A user, I WANT TO see content displayed using standard 16:9 covers, SO THAT browsing feels consistent and optimized for TV and large screens. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Pages & Lists |
| 3 | AVOD Content Lists | [UP] B2B2C Persona | AS A user, I WANT TO have content lists support background autoplay, SO THAT I get a quick preview of content without explicitly starting playback. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Pages & Lists |
| 4 | AVOD Content Lists | [UP] B2B Persona | AS A partner, I WANT TO configure AVOD content lists through a managed backend, SO THAT I can curate and organize the catalog without custom development. | 2026H1 MAM-WLA Lists |
| 5 | AVOD Content Lists | [UP] B2B Persona | AS A partner, I WANT TO define which lists support background autoplay, SO THAT I can balance discoverability with performance and editorial intent. | 2026H1 MAM-WLA Lists |
| 6 | AVOD Content Lists | [UP] RTV Persona | AS A platform owner, I WANT TO have AVOD content lists built on reusable, configurable components, SO THAT the same capabilities can scale across multiple white-label partners. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Pages & Lists |
| 7 | AVOD Content Lists | [UP] RTV Persona | AS A platform owner, I WANT TO have list behavior and autoplay rules be standardized but configurable, SO THAT we maintain UX consistency while supporting partner needs. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Pages & Lists |

## Example — AVOD Content Details

| # | Category | User Persona | User Story | Functional Definition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | AVOD Content Detail Pages | [UP] B2B2C Persona | AS A user, I WANT TO access a clear detail page for each AVOD title, SO THAT I understand what the content is before deciding to watch it. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Detail Pages |
| 2 | AVOD Content Detail Pages | [UP] B2B2C Persona | AS A user, I WANT TO see cast, directors, and genres on the detail page, SO THAT I can better understand the content and explore related titles. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Detail Pages |
| 3 | AVOD Content Detail Pages | [UP] B2B Persona | AS A partner, I WANT TO control which metadata fields appear on AVOD detail pages, SO THAT I can adapt the level of information to my audience and catalog quality. | 2026H1 MAM-WLA Pages |
| 4 | AVOD Content Detail Pages | [UP] RTV Persona | AS A platform owner, I WANT TO have AVOD detail pages follow a consistent, modular structure, SO THAT features can be reused across partners and devices. | [FD] 2026Q1 [CTV] VOD Detail Pages |
