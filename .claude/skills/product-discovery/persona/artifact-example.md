## Alex Martin — [UP] B2B Persona

**Personal quote:** "I need a streaming platform that supports all modern monetisation models and scales with our business, while keeping operational overhead manageable."

**Persona name:** Alex Martin (example)
**Persona role:** Business / Product Manager – Streaming Services

**Job description:**
1. Oversees evaluation, selection, and deployment of a white-label streaming solution
2. Drives product strategy, requirements, and go-to-market plans for OTT services across devices
3. Works with engineering, content, marketing, and external vendors to ensure delivery of a robust, scalable service

**Company:** Streaming Partner / Telco / Media Group / Broadcaster
**Company size:** 50–500 employees | **Industry:** Media & Entertainment, Telecommunications, Digital Services

**Demographic information:**
- Age: 30–50 | Gender: Male | Income: Mid-to-senior management | Education: Bachelor's or Master's | Residential: Urban

**Biography:** Alex is responsible for launching or enhancing a branded streaming service using a white-label solution. They must balance commercial goals (growth, monetisation, partnership value) with operational realities (content management, technical integration, reliability). Their work touches product strategy, technical onboarding, business development, and market expansion.

**Professional goals:**
- Deliver a robust streaming platform unifying AVOD, FAST, and TVOD monetisation models
- Enable fast time-to-market with strong branding and cross-device support
- Improve user engagement and retention with a smooth, familiar UX

**Motivators:**
- Monetisation flexibility: Support for AVOD, TVOD, and potential subscription tiers
- Brand control: Full visual and UX branding across platforms
- Operational efficiency: Unified backend reducing manual work

**Challenges:**
- Multi-model monetisation complexity
- Content and device complexity across TV, web, and mobile
- Operational fragmentation from legacy systems

**Sources of information:**
- White-label platform documentation
- Industry reports on OTT best practices
- Vendor RFP responses and benchmark comparisons

## Marco Ferretti — [UP] B2B Persona (Engineer/Integrator)

**Personal quote:** "Every platform looks great in the sales deck. I've learned to judge a vendor by their documentation — because that's what I'll be living with for the next two years, not the pitch."

**Persona name:** Marco Ferretti (example)
**Persona role:** Integration Engineer / OTT Platform Engineer

**Biography:** Marco is the technical counterpart to Alex Martin. Alex owns the commercial decision; Marco owns the technical feasibility that either validates or gates it. His job starts where the pitch deck ends — he reads documentation, opens DevTools, maps data schemas, and validates that the platform's integration surface matches what his company's existing systems can supply.

**Professional goals:**
- Understand the complete surface area of WLA screens, features, and external service touchpoints before committing to a timeline
- Map WLA's data and service requirements to his company's existing stack
- Validate that all platform integration points are documented clearly enough to implement without vendor support calls

**Motivators:**
- Clarity over marketing language
- Self-service evaluation
- Predictability: versioned changelog that signals breaking changes before they reach production

**Challenges:**
- Documentation gap: WLA documentation written for business buyers, not technical evaluators
- No platform surface map: cannot scope integration effort without a screen inventory
- Timeline pressure from commercial process he didn't own

## Emma Rossi — [UP] B2B2C Persona

**Personal quote:** "I just want to watch — whether that's free content between ads, a show I subscribe to, or a fight I paid for tonight. Whatever I'm watching, I expect it to work perfectly and feel like it was made for me."

**Persona name:** Emma Rossi (example)
**Persona role:** Streaming Platform End User – Casual & Engaged Viewer

**Biography:** Emma is a digital-native viewer who consumes content across all monetization modes. Her expectations shift significantly depending on what she's watching and what she paid for it. She values discoverability, personalization, and platform reliability as baseline — and elevates her quality expectations proportionally to her financial investment.

**Motivators:**
- Convenience & accessibility across devices
- Variety & flexibility: mix of AVOD, TVOD, and FAST in one app
- Personalization: recommendations, watchlists, remembered progress

**Challenges:**
- Fragmented experience across multiple apps
- Difficulty discovering content on large catalogues
- Unclear guidance on which content is free vs. paid

## Giulia Rossi — [UP] RTV Persona

**Personal quote:** "I ensure that our white-label solutions are scalable, reliable, and flexible so our partners can deliver the best experience to their users."

**Persona name:** Giulia Rossi (example)
**Persona role:** Rakuten Enterprise Product Manager – White-Label Solutions & Platform Features

**Biography:** Giulia bridges product strategy and partner needs, overseeing feature definition, backlog prioritisation, and roadmap execution. She focuses on ensuring that Rakuten's white-label technology supports AVOD, TVOD, and FAST models while maintaining operational efficiency and technical reliability.

**Motivators:**
- Partner satisfaction and revenue maximisation
- Technical reliability across devices
- Scalability: handling multiple partners efficiently

**Challenges:**
- Complex partner UI/UX and branding requirements
- Integrating monetisation models in a unified workflow
- Coordinating multiple teams to deliver features on time
