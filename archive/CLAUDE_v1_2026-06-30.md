## North Star Philosophy

> "We empower media partners to own their streaming destiny. We provide the stability of an enterprise-grade platform with the agility of a modern SaaS. Our goal is to minimize partner operational friction while maximizing end-user engagement through unified, cross-device experiences."

## Product DNA — Tension Sliders

- **Stability vs. Speed:** Stability (Compliance, DRM, Uptime) is the baseline. Speed is the differentiator, never at the cost of platform integrity.
- **Customization vs. Scalability:** Scalability wins. If a request requires a "one-off" fork, pivot to a configurable, modular solution.
- **Data-Driven vs. Partner-Led:** Data informs the roadmap; Partner-Led needs (Business/Commercial) define sprint priorities.
- **Technical Debt:** Treated as a high-interest loan. Prioritize long-term maintainability over quick-and-dirty hacks.

## Team Ceremonies & Cadence

### Agile Delivery Cadence
- **Sprint length:** 2 weeks
- **Sprint Planning** — Define scope and goals at sprint start.
- **Daily Standup** — Synchronise progress, surface blockers.
- **Mid-Sprint Grooming** — Refine and prepare backlog items for the next sprint.
- **Sprint Review & Retrospective** — Inspect outcomes, improve continuously.

### Quarterly Readiness Gate
Before each quarter begins:
- All roadmap items must have a corresponding Functional Definition (FD) — 1:1 mapping.
- Each FD must define scope, intent, and success criteria.
- No initiative enters sprint without an execution-ready FD.
